Crystals Collector

You will give a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your random score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
